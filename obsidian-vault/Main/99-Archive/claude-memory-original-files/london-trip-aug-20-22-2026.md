### Westminster Walking Tour (9:30 - 12:30, ~5 km, hotel to hotel)
Based on Guri in London "Essential London" tour (guriinlondon.com/essencial/), extended with Whitehall.

**Leg 1: Westminster monuments (1h 50min)**
1. Westminster Abbey exterior (25 min): West Front towers, North Door rose window, Tomb of Unknown Warrior plaque, St. Margaret's Church next door
2. Parliament Square statues (25 min): Churchill (pigeon-proof head), Mandela, Gandhi, Lincoln, Millicent Fawcett (only woman, "Courage calls to courage everywhere")
3. Big Ben + Houses of Parliament (25 min): Corner of Bridge St + Victoria Embankment for ground photo, Cromwell statue
4. Westminster Bridge (30 min): cross halfway for skyline photo, touch Boudicca statue at corner

**Leg 2: Whitehall (45 min)**
5. Cenotaph (10 min): Plain Portland stone, Lutyens 1920, no religious symbols by design
6. 10 Downing Street (10 min): View through gates, armed police, don't photograph officers' faces
7. Horse Guards (15 min): Mounted Household Cavalry change on the hour, walk through arch to Horse Guards Parade (Trooping the Colour venue), black mark at "2" on clock = hour of Charles I beheading
8. Banqueting House (10 min): Only surviving piece of old Whitehall Palace, Charles I beheaded outside Jan 30, 1649, plaque marks spot

**Leg 3: Changing of the Guard (30 min)**
- 10:45-10:55: Walk through St James's Park east end to Birdcage Walk
- 10:55-11:30: Buckingham Palace ceremony
- Stand on Victoria Memorial steps (not gates, six rows deep)
- Royal Standard flying = King home, Union Jack = away
- Check schedule day before: https://householddivision.org.uk/changing-the-guard-calendar
- Backup if cancelled: Horse Guards mounted change 11:00 Mon-Sat

**Leg 4: St James's Park loop back (45 min)**
9. Slow walk north path along lake
10. Blue Bridge in middle: best view (Palace one way, Horse Guards turrets the other)
11. Duck Island Cottage: pelicans fed 2:30 PM daily
12. Exit Storey's Gate, walk to hotel

## Day 2: Friday Aug 21 (Tower + Palace)

- 08:00: Breakfast at hotel
- 08:45: Tube St. James's Park to Tower Hill (Circle Line, 8 stops, 17 min)
- 09:30-12:30: **Tower of London**
  - Crown Jewels FIRST (before queue builds)
  - Free Beefeater tour at 10:00 (1 hour, hilarious)
  - White Tower (Norman keep 1078, armor)
  - Ravens in courtyard (legend: kingdom falls if they leave)
- 12:30-13:00: Walk Tower Bridge (use pavement, not glass walkway)
- 13:00-13:45: **Lunch Borough Market**
  - Bagel Box: salt beef bagel
  - Richard Haward's: oysters
  - Bread Ahead: brownie
- 13:45: Walk to London Bridge station, Jubilee Line to Green Park (5 stops, 11 min), walk through Green Park 8 min
- 14:30-16:45: **Buckingham Palace State Rooms**
  - Entry south side via Buckingham Palace Road (Ambassadors' Court)
  - Audio guide included (Charles III narrates portions)
  - 19 rooms: Throne Room, Ballroom (largest), Picture Gallery (Rembrandt, Vermeer, Canaletto), Grand Staircase
  - Exit through Garden with palace back views
- 16:45-17:30: Walk back via Green Park to hotel (12 min)
- 18:00-19:30: Pub drinks + dinner: The Speaker (4 min, quiet) or St Stephen's Tavern (8 min, opposite Big Ben)
- Optional 20:30: West End theater
  - Wicked at Apollo Victoria (8 min walk from hotel)
  - Les Misérables (Sondheim), Lion King (Lyceum)
  - Book via TodayTix app or official theater sites
