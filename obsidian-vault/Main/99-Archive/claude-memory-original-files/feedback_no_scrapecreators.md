Do NOT use `scrape-creators-agent` for social media fetching. Use `browser-agent` (Lightpanda) instead.

**Why:** Roberto deprecated scrape-creators-agent in favor of Lightpanda browser-agent for all social media content fetching.

**How to apply:** When asked to check Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X, or any social media URL, use browser-agent (Lightpanda) to fetch the content. Never launch scrape-creators-agent.
